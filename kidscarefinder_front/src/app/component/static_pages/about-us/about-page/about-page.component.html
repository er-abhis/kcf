What is Kids Care Finder (KCF)?

What is Kids Care Finder?

The search for reliable child care that meets your family’s unique needs can be a never-ending and time-consuming challenge. Even after you find the perfect fit, your family’s needs constantly change as your kids grow and develop, leaving you in a perpetual process that restarts each time you look for a new form of care or extra curricular activity.

It can take weeks of searching, dozens of phone calls, countless interviews and tours, and still you may not have found the perfect fit.

Now you can find any type of childcare for all of your family’s needs in one place. From a Nanny to help with your newborn baby, to a Daycare or Preschool for your bright and curious toddler, or even sports and rec activities or a summer camp for your growing teenager, Kids Care Finder can help you find the optimal childcare fit for each child in your family.

Kids Care Finder isn’t just helping make life easier for families in search of care, but for our providers too! When you sign up to be a provider with Kids Care Finder, we make it possible to offer online enrollment, eliminating hours of painful and tedious paperwork. You can also accept online applications, and easily manage a waitlist on our site when you do not currently have space available. With less administrative work and less time responding to inquiries about your business, you can spend more time focused on the children you care for.

Why Kids Care Finder (KCF)?

Why Kids Care Finder?

  • No monthly subscription fee for users
  • We do not take any percentage of tuition or enrollment fees from providers.
  • We offer an industry leading number of provider types, and will continuously be adding more until all childcare needs are covered!
  • Our unique search results table displays a significant amount of key information on each provider on one screen, saving countless hours spent calling and emailing dozens of providers to ask the same basic questions
  • Providers can dramatically reduce their administrative load by managing their waitlist, online applications and online enrollment via the Provider Portal
  • Our patent pending functionality allows much more than just messaging between users and providers. Additional features include:
    • Users and providers can save precious time writing messaging back and forth with our quick messages
  • Filter providers based on specific needs or requirements
  • Join provider’s waitlists and know what number you are
  • Use our integrated online application template to easily apply for various provider types

About the Founder

About the Founder

Kids Care Finder started in Carlsbad, CA by Olivia Rutman, a working mom of three boys. Her experience with finding safe and reliable childcare was riddled with obstacles that made it almost impossible to find suitable solutions to all the needs of her growing family. From childcare providers to extracurricular activities, it seemed that Olivia always had to jump through countless hoops and spend weeks searching for care for each of her boys, each time their needs changed. Even after finding a potential fit, with each new program, she would start from square one with an email, call, interview, tour, and application - then repeating the cycle all over again if it didn’t work out.

When a fit was finally found, she was sent home with hours of tedious paperwork to complete, which is no small ask for a fast moving working parent. It was an incredibly time intensive and mentally exhausting process, and what seemed like a never-ending cycle. After talking with other families, she realized this is an unfortunate commonality that almost all parents face, on top of the other countless challenges that come with raising their kids.

Some of whom she spoke to were still struggling with their search for reliable care, and in many cases didn’t even know where to begin looking. While on maternity leave with baby number three, and after looking for new care for what felt like the hundredth time, Olivia knew there needed to be an easier way. She jumped to work and created Kids Care Finder to help families everywhere quickly and easily find the right childcare fit for their family.
