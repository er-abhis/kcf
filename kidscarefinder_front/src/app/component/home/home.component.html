Our Mission

Kids Care Finder is dedicated to making the process of finding any type of childcare as simple, fast, and hassle-free as possible for both our families and providers. We connect our users with all types of care categories, and make it easy to determine which providers are the right fit for each family's unique needs. Prospective families are also able to view ratings and reviews on our providers, and see a significant amount of information on each provider on one screen, saving dozens of hours calling and emailing providers to ask the same basic questions.

Using Kids Care Finder, our providers will also have access to quickly communicate with prospective parents, easily manage a waitlist, accept online applications, and receive required enrollment documents 100% electronically.
