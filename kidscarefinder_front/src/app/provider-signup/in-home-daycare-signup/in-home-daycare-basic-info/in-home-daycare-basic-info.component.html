Let’s get started!

To become a provider with Kids Care Finder, you will need to complete our unique provider questionnaire. This will help provide users with all of the information they need to find a provider that meets their family’s unique needs.

As a Daycare provider, you will have the option to:

  • Send messages to-and-from users from your Message Center
  • Easily manage a waitlist for your Daycare
  • Accept and review online applications
  • Offer enrollment to users that is fast, simple, and paper-free
  • Fill openings quickly with our Search Result Optimization service

Watch this short walkthrough video on our provider questionnaire

! Note: This questionnaire takes approximately 15-20 minutes to complete. If you cannot complete the full questionnaire upon starting, you will have the option to save your progress and finish the remaining steps at a later time
