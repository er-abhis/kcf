Let’s get started!
To be come a provider with Kids Care Finder, you will need to complete our unique provider questionnaire.
This will help provide users with all of the information they need to find a provider that meets their family’s unique needs.
As a Babysitter provider, you will have the option to:
  • Send messages to-and-from users from your Message Center
  • Easily manage a waitlist when you are not accepting new bookings
  • Review and accept online booking requests
  • Fill openings quickly with our Search Result Optimization service
  • Have your own customized provider page - like having your own website!
